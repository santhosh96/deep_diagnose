Physical Examination

Steps for medical procedures

  • Scraping of diseased region using blunt tool (slide).
  • Introducing 1 drop of 10% KOH to the slide.
  • Introducing 2 drops of 1% CSB (Chicago Sky Blue) stain.
  • Cover the slide using the cover slip and wait for 15 minutes.

Image Capturing

Observing under the microscope

  • Once the specimen is ready for observation, carefully place the slide onto the observation tray of microscope.
  • Set/Install magnification to 40X.
  • Locate the areas that shows Spores and Hyphae.
  • Once located, capture the image using smartphone camera using camera mount.
